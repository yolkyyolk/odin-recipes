Ramen

Description

A delicious Japanese noodle soup dish, usually made with wheat noodles in a flavored broth. Seasoned with soysauce, and topped with ingredients of your choice. Personal favorites include pork, seaweed, bean sprouts, and green onion. Highly customizable.

We are going for the minimalist version here, so if you want to get fancy, look elsewhere.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles: can be fresh, or maybe you want to spice up a package of Maruchan/Top Ramen.
  • Broth
  • Miso or soysauce
  • Protein: both pork and tofu are tasty options.
  • Seasame oil
  • Toppings: bean sprouts, green onion, egg, seawood, or whatever garnish you would enjoy.

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Make the sauce/use broth of choice: usually involves mixing broth flavor, such as beef or a flavor packet, with water.
  3. Saute any added (optional) veggie toppings. If adding pork or egg, have cooked and ready to throw in after veggies are cooked.
  4. Combine flavors: add soysauce if using, red pepper flakes, seasame oil, or a little brown sugar for a bit of sweetness. Experiment.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the sauce pan, along with any prepared proteins.
  6. Add garnish, such as green onion or bean sprouts.
  7. Serve, and enjoy!
